Welcome to the public Legislative Records Request generator. Please read the disclaimer below before getting started...

When you're ready, just fill in your name at the left, and select the records request categories of interest to you. You will be able to add or change requests before sending if you have additional or completely different areas of interest.

Once you've selected your categories of interest (if any), click the "Preview Request Email" to see a complete records request letter ready for sending.

When you are ready to send, click "Generate Email Request" and your default email client should open. If you have any problems, just copy and paste the preview email into an email, and send to the address inidicated below.
